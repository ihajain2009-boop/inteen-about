About Us

At InTeen, we believe every teenager deserves the chance to discover who they are beyond textbooks and board exams. We are a non-profit organisation dedicated to helping high school students across India find meaningful internship opportunities — opportunities that allow them to explore their interests, understand real-world working environments, and build confidence at a young age.

Growing up in a system where marks often overshadow potential, we realised how limited the exposure is for students who want to learn by doing, not just studying. InTeen was created to bridge this gap by connecting motivated teenagers with companies and startups that are open to guiding and mentoring young minds. Our mission is simple: create a space where students can step into the professional world early, grow through experience, and discover their true capabilities.

Starting InTeen was never about building an organisation — it was about solving a problem we felt deeply ourselves. As high school students, we often wished for guidance, exposure, and the chance to see what the world outside school really looked like. We realised that thousands of students across India felt the same way.

InTeen grew from that desire to make a meaningful difference. We want to give teenagers a place where they feel seen, supported, and inspired to try new things without fear. Our hope is that every student who passes through InTeen walks away with a little more confidence, clarity, and courage than before.

~ Ridima Gupta & Iha Jain, Co-Founders, InTeen

Our Vision

We envision an India where teenagers aren’t defined solely by numbers on a report card. Instead, they are shaped by experiences, interactions, and opportunities that build leadership, empathy, and ambition. Our dream is to help create a generation that’s not only academically strong but also curious, confident, and future-ready.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower young individuals through hands-on exposure and real-world learning. By connecting high school students with forward-thinking organisations, InTeen aims to cultivate a culture where learning extends far beyond the classroom. We strive to give teenagers the guidance, support, and opportunities they need to understand their strengths, explore their ambitions, and grow into impactful contributors to society.
